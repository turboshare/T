What is TRUE POI?

TRUE POI is an enhanced implementation of the POWH3D algorithm with additional features including bonus pools, anti-whale protection, and a sophisticated dividend system. It uses price asymmetry to create powerful holding incentives.

How does the dividend system work?

10% of all transaction volume (buys, sells, transfers, reinvests) is distributed proportionally to all POI holders as dividends. You can withdraw these dividends at any time or reinvest them to compound your position.

Why is there a higher spread between the buy and sell price?

It's part of how the POI system works. When you buy, the price is increased by multiplying the buy price by 1.1x, and when you sell, the sell price is multiplied by 0.90x.
- That extra difference (called the spread) doesn't just sit in the contract -- it goes into a bonus pool.
- Once the bonus pool reaches the threshold level, it automatically gets shared with all POI holders.
- It's a way to reward people who stick around and support the ecosystem.

How is the bonus pool funded?

The bonus pool comes from the difference in price between buying and selling (the 1.10x and 0.90x effect).
- You pay a bit more when buying -- that extra goes to the pool.
- You get a bit less when selling -- that also adds to the pool.
- These funds get collected and later paid out to holders by the contract itself.
- This setup is separate from the usual 10% fee you pay when entering or leaving.

What is the bonus pool mechanism?

The price asymmetry system (buyers pay 11% more, sellers receive 10% less) creates this bonus pool. Every transaction feeds it with POL that gets distributed to ALL POI holders when the threshold is reached. The threshold mechanics are simple: it started at 15 POL and decreases by 0.01 POL with every transaction on the contract. When it reaches 0.1 POL minimum, it automatically resets to 1 POL and continues decreasing by 0.01 POL per transaction - this cycle repeats forever. For example, if the threshold is at 5 POL and the pool accumulates 5.5 POL, all 5.5 POL gets distributed proportionally to POI holders. After 490 transactions, the threshold would hit 0.1 POL, reset to 1 POL, and the cycle continues. This creates frequent, predictable distributions that reward patience - those who hold through multiple distributions earn significantly more than early sellers who panic. It's mathematically designed to create diamond hands!

How does anti-whale protection work?

Daily limits of 10 POL are enforced on sells, withdrawals, and transfers to prevent large holders from draining the contract or manipulating the market. This protects smaller investors.

What are the fees?

10% dividend fee on all transactions, 4% referral bonus (when applicable), and 1% development fee on purchases. The price asymmetry creates additional value for the bonus pool.

How does the referral system work?

When someone buys through your referral link and you hold the minimum required POI, you earn 4% of their purchase as a bonus. This encourages community growth.

Is there a referral fee for reinvest?

No, reinvesting your dividends does not trigger the 4% referral fee, but the 1% dev fee still applies. When you hit the reinvest button, the contract executes a buy transaction with your dividend balance but sets the referral address to zero. This is because the contract doesn't store who originally referred you - it would be too gas-expensive and complex to track every user's original referrer permanently on-chain. So on reinvest, you pay: 10% dividend fee (distributed to all holders) + 1% dev fee = 11% total fees. The absence of referral fees on reinvest is a technical limitation turned feature - it incentivizes holders to compound their positions rather than withdraw, strengthening the ecosystem while keeping the contract simple and gas-efficient.
